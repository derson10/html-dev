EXECUTOR PROFILE

Executors are active, competitive and dynamic people.
They are not afraid to take risks and face challenges. They are hardworking, have an enormous physical disposition and demonstrate a lot of determination and perseverance. What characterizes this profile is their willpower. tends to the logical/deductive and their imagination and judgment are usually balanced. They enjoy challenges and obstacles stimulate them to act more and more. They tend to execute first, then think about how to do it.

They have autonomy, independence and know how to impose themselves on others.
They are self-confident and have leadership characteristics. A point to develop is that they can be authoritarian and somewhat inflexible, because they believe in their point of view, and this leads them to fight for your ideals. Therefore, this profile is suitable for quick tasks that require initiative and determination. It is important to understand that for this profile there are no obstacles, and if there are, he will overcome them without measuring efforts.

HIGH PERFORMER

BEHAVIOURS: Self-confident, initiative, accepts challenges, is competitive, individualistic and enterprising.

NEEDS: Result, competition, problem solving, freedom to act individually, recognition for your ideas, control of your own activities, opportunity to prove your ability.

LOW PERFORMER

BEHAVIOURS: Cooperative and pleasant, accepts company policies, seeks harmony, risk averse, accepts things as they are, works as a team.

NEEDS: Support and absence of individual competition, team recognition, understanding supervision, encouragement, need harmony, frictionless environment and not having to make risky decisions.

LOOK THE TES

DETERMINATION
This subfactor appears when the Executor is considerably taller than the Communicator. If he does not have any Communicator sociability, then this person will have little interest in personal matters, so determination is prominent. This person has an objective view in relation to things and has focus, not giving up on their goals. Because they need to see the result of their work, this person can compromise on quality and details.

INDEPENDENCES
This subfactor appears when the Executor profile is higher than the Analyst profile. Because this individual is more independent, being subjected to rules and procedures will frustrate him. works in a more isolated way, very typical of an entrepreneur.

AUTOMOTIVE
Self-motivation appears when there is no need for security and passivity from the Planner (high). Then action becomes a key ingredient in this subfactor. This person needs to be active all the time and is very impatient with those who are unwilling or unable to keep up with its pace. It seeks to pursue its ambitions and goals hard.
